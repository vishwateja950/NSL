The Ethos of NSL Infratech

In the present competitive and dynamic real estate sector, very few players have such an enviable track record as NSL Infratech. NSL Infratech has set standards in the building industry through architectural integrity, technological superiority, constant innovation, and flawless execution thereby creating landmark commercial and residential projects pan India. With 20 landmark commercial and residential projects, NSL Infratech leads the way in work ethics and customer commitment. NSL Infratech is a part of the NSL (Nuziveedu Seeds Limited) Group, a dynamic group established in 1974, with well-directed operations headed by experienced leaders, adoption of industry best practices, extensive research, and emphasis on quality.

Mission & Vision

To become a leading and trusted real estate company with a reputation to develop sustainable properties that improves quality of life. To achieve the status of preferred real estate and infrastructure company in India by the year 2030 and provide exceptional real estate services to meet the unique needs and aspirations of our customers.

Providing quality space at Competitive costs, delivered on time to customers.

Leadership

Mr. M Prabhakar Rao

Chairman & Managing Director

Mr. M Prabhakar Rao, the Chairman and Managing Director of the NSL Group is a Gold Medalist from Benaras Hindu University in M.Sc (Agri) and stood 2nd during his graduation. Since 1982 when he joined the Company, his able leadership has enabled the group to grow manifold, diversifying into other fields like Power, Textiles & Infrastructure in the process. Thanks to his sound knowledge base and business acumen, Nuziveedu Seeds is now the largest privately held seed company in India while the cotton seeds division holds the majority share of the Indian market. In recognition of his contribution, he has been elected President- Indian Seed Industry Association, Member- Central Seed Certification Board, Member- Governing Board, Acharya N. G. Ranga Agricultural University. Under his Aegis, the seeds company had been awarded the best agri company for the year 2008 by Biospectrum. He has been elected as advisor to the Government of India and the State Governments on all matters related with Seeds. The flagship, Nuziveedu Seeds Pvt Limited is also the Winner of DSIR – National Award, 2002 instituted by Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India for the R & D achievements of the company.

Aishwariya M.

Executive Director

Mrs. M Aishwarya, Executive Director of NSL Infra, is at the helm of our ever-growing real estate business, driving innovation, growth, and success. Mrs. Aishwarya holds her master’s degree in business administration from University of Cambridge, United Kingdom with experience in investment banking sector. As an Executive Director, she sets the strategic direction for our real estate ventures. Her visionary leadership has been instrumental in our organization’s ability to identify emerging trends, capitalize on new market opportunities and consistently achieve outstanding results. Her commitment to excellence, coupled with deep knowledge of the markets, has earned her a distinguished reputation as a leader in the field. Mrs. Aishwarya believes in the power of collaboration and teamwork. Her leadership style promotes an environment where creativity and innovation flourish. Her comprehensive understanding of the real estate industry, ability to navigate complex transactions, negotiate and close deals has been instrumental in our continued success.

Cdr. Vinod Nathan (Retd)

Chief Operating Officer

Cdr. Vinod Nathan (Retd), joined the company in October 2008 after working for 22 years with the Indian Navy. An Electrical Engineer holding Masters in Nuclear Engineering & Technology (IIT, Kanpur) & a certificate holder in Management (IIM, Lucknow), he brings with him the organizational capabilities imbibed from the Navy. He has handled numerous projects from conceptualization to induction in the past and is responsible for the management of all assets and joint developments of the Company.
