NSL Divyasree Orion

Raidurg, Hyderabad

NRION, an IT / ITES SEZ is spread across 40 acres in Raidurg, in close proximity to Gachibowli and Hitech city – the IT hub, and Banjara Hills – the commercial hub, making it one of the prime locations in Hyderabad. The entire SEZ has been designed by internationally renowned architects. Construction of two IT buildings of about 4 million sft. is completed. Excellent connectivity, proximity to Hitech city, financial district and the surrounding social amenities and infrastructure makes it a right destination for top global players in the IT / ITES industries.
