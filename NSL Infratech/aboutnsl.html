About NSL Group

Nuziveedu Seeds Limited was established in mid 70s with the business objective of providing high quality seeds and to contribute for the
agricultural economy of the country. Thereafter the Company diversified strategically into a variety of businesses.

Presently, the NSL Group is led by Mandava Holdings Private Limited, the Holding Company, NSL Group has diversified into various business
sectors such as infrastructure, power, textiles, sugars apart from the flagship business of seeds. Please click the link for details :
www.nslgroup.co.in

Group Companies Overview

NUZIVEEDU SEEDS LTD

Nuziveedu Seeds Ltd is India's Largest Bt cotton seed company (in terms of
volumes of seed packets sold in 2012-13)*. It creates, produces, and
distributes hybrid and varietal seed products. It is the flagship business of the NSL Group and one of the most trusted seed companies in India.

NSL TEXTILES LTD

NSL Textiles Ltd is one of the largest and fully integrated Textile Companies in India, equipped with state-of-art machinery and promoted with the concept of "Cotton to Clothing". The manufacturing operations are spread over 5 locations and provide massive employment opportunities in the Textilesector with 7000 employees in Andhra Pradesh.

NSL RENEWABLE POWER PVT LTD

NSL Renewable Power Private Limited is one of the fastest-growing and independent renewable power companies with interests in Wind farms, Hydel power and Biomass plants. The company follows an unparalleled business model in each of the verticals and thus evolved as an independent power producer playing a vital role in satisfying India’s energy needs through innovation and sustainable methods.
