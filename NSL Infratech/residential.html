NSL East County

Uppal, Hyderabad

NSL EAST COUNTY AT UPPAL Spread over 5.7 acres is the pride of Eastern Vector of Hyderabad. Live a premium lifestyle in an uncluttered, pristine environment. NSL East County offers the best of both worlds. A location that is near a commercial hub and an easy commute to all your areas of interest, East County is as close as you can get to it all but offers a lifestyle that is away from the chaos of city life. From tree-lined walkways, walking and jogging paths, solar street lighting, organic waste converters, adequate parking areas, sewage and water treatment plants, garbage chutes, etc., the entire community has been planned for green, clean living.

Orion Villas

Gachibowli, Hyderabad

Located near Orion SEZ, 10.5 acres of land are exclusively allotted for the residential development of 62 luxurious villas. The location is neighboring Jubilee Hills and Hi-Tech City on one side and Gachibowli and Financial District on the other side. Proximity to Outer Ring Road provides excellent connectivity to the International Airport. One can find the perfect meaning of the walk-to-work concept in this gated community as it is being developed adjacent to the 6 million square feet of IT space. The lifestyle villas located on a plateau offer an excellent view of the lakes nearby, and the serene atmosphere prevalent makes living here a pleasure.

Serene Woods

Hennur Road, Bangalore

Serene Woods is an exclusive residential enclave comprising 61 Row Houses in a lush green environment off Hennur Road. Serene Woods is just 14 kms from MG Road, 7 kms from Manyatha Tech Park, 6 kms from Outer Ring Road & 28 kms to Bangalore Airport. This makes it the perfect vantage point in terms of proximity for those with travel time on their mind, a jackpot for those with a view on the return on their investment and an escape to serenity for those whose love nature.
